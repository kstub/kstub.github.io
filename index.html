About Me

I am a Simons Postdoctoral Fellow in the Institute for Pure and Applied Mathematics at UCLA.

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection between mathematics, physics, and computer science. In particular, I am interested in developing fast algorithms for high dimensional problems with a focus on quantum chemistry and materials science. I am particularly interested in leveraging insights from physics to design provably fast algorithms.
